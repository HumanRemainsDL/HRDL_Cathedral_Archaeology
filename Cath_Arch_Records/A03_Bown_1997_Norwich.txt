Undisturbed medieval inhumations of late 10th and 11th century date. These are located outside the cathedral in the angle between the North Nave exterior wall and the North West Transept wall. Int he late 10th and 11th century the area was used as a graveyard; after the construction of the cathedral it appears to have been used as a yard.
