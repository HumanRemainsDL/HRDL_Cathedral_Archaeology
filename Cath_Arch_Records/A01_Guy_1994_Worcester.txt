Skeleton of an adult male of 8th or 9th century date which had been exhumed and reburied in a small stone cist in a disarticulated state in the late 11th or 12th century. The cist post-dates the painted plaster in the medieval chapel (completed by 1092). The lid of the cist was replaced in the 13th century (see separate entry).
