Exhumation of a medieval inhumation (probably a priest), and reburial in a pit at the east end of the Nave of the Lady Chapel in the 14th or 15th century. The pit is lcoated in front of the chancel arch. Traces of pewter were discovered with the disarticulated skeleton, probably suggestive of the reburial of a chalice and/or paten.
