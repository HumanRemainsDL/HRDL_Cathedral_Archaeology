Opening of a stone coffin during reflooring of the Chapter House in 1889. The workmen saw within the coffin a body clothed in robes which looked like episcopal robes and without a head. This was assumed to be the body of St Hugh of Avalon. See additional entry for tomb openings in 1955 which attempted to relocate this burial.
