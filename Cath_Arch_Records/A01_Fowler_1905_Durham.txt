Opening of the grave of Richard of Bury, Bishop of Durham (1333-1345) during the installation of a new grave-cover in 1903. The grave was filled with earth and stones presumed to have been deposited during the relaying of the floor in the 19th century (see separate entry). Beneath the rubble was the skeleton of Bishop Richard de Bury; this was observed and left in-situ.
