Disturbance of a 12th and a 14th century inhumation probably during reflooring in the 18th century. The 14th century burial was in a stone coffin and had a chalice and paten. The chalice and paten had apparently been lifted and replaced in the 18th century as they both lay on infill material which had filled the coffin. See additional entry for further information.
