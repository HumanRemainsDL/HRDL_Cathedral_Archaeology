Intense area of medieval burials beginning in the middle Saxon period and ceasing after the construction of the Processional Way possibly in the 13th century. The central part of the site was completely disturbed by the footings for a Victorian organ blower while a boiler cellar occupied the north-western corner of the site. Reburied in Cathedral precinct (see additional entry).
