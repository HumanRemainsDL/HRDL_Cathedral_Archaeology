Eight 19th century ossuary / charnel pits containing disarticulated human remains, mainly skulls and long bones. The remains are likely to be those disturbed during the clearing out of lairs in the South West tower during the demolition process (completed 1846). Several of the skulls had been cut for autopsy or anatomical study.
