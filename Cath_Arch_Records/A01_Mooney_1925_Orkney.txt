Four undisturbed medieval inhumations found in a row along the main axis of the Choir. One grave contained a pewter chalice, a pewter paten, and a pewter crozier. These were removed from the grave and are now in the National Museums of Scotland. The report does not give further information on the subsequent treatment of the skeletal remains.
